Event Overview
Attention Techies!

We would really like to thank all of you for always being supportive and encouraging us to keep bringing better learning opportunities to the ITC Community.

IT Club invites you to join its first two-day hands-on workshop on web development with a highly proficient and skilled web and software developer, Mr. Akash Raj Singh. Currently, he serves as a software developer at Amazon. He is also an active student mentor in the same field.

The workshop will include everything from bare basics of HTML, CSS and Javascript to designing, developing and deploying a functional web app. It will be a highly interactive and knowledgeable workshop, with an interesting quiz competition in the end. The winners will be awarded prizes worth S$50!

To save your seat, please fill the form (scan the QR code/ browse https://simitclub.com / Link in Bio) and feel free to get your friends along! The event is open for all the interested learners across the planet.
